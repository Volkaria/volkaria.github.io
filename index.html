About Me

My name is Brian Tran, and I am an aspiring game designer. This portfolio is a representation of all that I've learned from studying games. I tend to want to do a lot of scripting as it feels most natural for me.

University Projects

  • Game Testing

    During TeSPA's board game nights, other people would bring their own pre released games for us to test on.

  • Game Design Class

    The class was based on creating board games from scratch. From the rules to the pieces themselves, everything was produced by a group of students.

  • Classes taken during University

    Game Design 1, Project Management, Design 1, Game Studies, Fundamentals of Game Development, and Level Design
